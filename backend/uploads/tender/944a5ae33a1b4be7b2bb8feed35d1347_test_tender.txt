Scope of Work:
Construction of 4-lane cement concrete road from Pune to Nashik (Talegaon to Igatpuri Section), approximately 68 km stretch. Work includes earthwork, subgrade preparation, granular sub-base, wet mix macadam, cement concrete pavement, road markings, median development, drainage channels, and installation of highway signage.

Technical Specifications:
- Cement Concrete Pavement of M-40 grade minimum
- Pavement thickness: 300mm with dowel bars
- Granular sub-base: 200mm compacted layer
- Storm water drains: RCC box drains as per IRC:SP:42
- Road geometry alignment as per IRC:73 standards
- Bituminous surface for shoulders

Eligibility Criteria:
The bidder must satisfy ALL of the following conditions:
1. Must have successfully completed at least two similar road/highway projects of value not less than INR 25 Crores each in the last seven years.
2. Average Annual Turnover of the bidder must not be less than INR 50 Crores over the last three consecutive financial years.
3. Net worth must be positive as per the latest audited balance sheet.
4. The bidder must be registered with any State PWD / NHAI / CPWD.

Mandatory Certifications:
- ISO 9001:2015 certification is mandatory
- ISO 14001:2015 certification is mandatory
- NSIC or MSME registration preferred

Financial Requirement:
- Earnest Money Deposit (EMD): INR 75 Lakhs (demand draft)
