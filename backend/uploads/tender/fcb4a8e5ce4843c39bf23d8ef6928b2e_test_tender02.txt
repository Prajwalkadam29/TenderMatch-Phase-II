Scope of Work:
Construction of a minor RCC bridge over the Tapi river tributary in Surat District, including the development of 2.5 km of adjoining approach roads. The project includes earthwork, grading operations, construction of RCC piers and abutments, laying of bituminous approach roads, and installation of storm water drainage systems along the approaches.


Technical Specifications:
- RCC structures using M-35 grade concrete minimum
- Bridge design and alignment as per standard IRC bridge codes
- Bituminous pavement for approach roads with a 150mm granular sub-base
- Storm water drainage using pre-cast RCC pipes
- Installation of reflective highway signage and crash barriers


Eligibility Criteria:
The bidder must satisfy ALL of the following conditions:

1. Must have successfully completed at least one bridge or highway construction project of a value not less than INR 15 Crores in the last five years.
2. Average Annual Turnover of the bidder must not be less than INR 40 Crores over the last three consecutive financial years.
3. Net worth must be positive as per the latest audited balance sheet.
4. The bidder must be registered with the State PWD or CPWD.


Mandatory Certifications:
- ISO 9001:2015 certification is mandatory
- ISO 45001:2018 (Occupational Health & Safety) is highly preferred


Financial Requirement:
- Earnest Money Deposit (EMD): INR 45 Lakhs (bank guarantee or demand draft)
- Performance Security: 5% of the total contract value
- Bid Validity: 120 days from the date of submission
