Issuing Authority: Pune Smart City Development Corporation Limited (PSCDCL)
Portal: mahatenders.gov.in
Published Date: 19 March 2026
Corrigendum: None

Scope of Work:
Upgradation of existing urban roads to "Smart Roads" in the Baner-Balewadi zone (approximately 5 km network). The scope includes laying cement concrete pavement, constructing underground utility ducts, developing pedestrian walkways, and installing advanced traffic signaling infrastructure and smart poles at major intersections.


Technical Specifications:
- Cement Concrete Pavement of M-40 grade
- Construction of RCC box ducts for underground utilities
- Pedestrian walkways using anti-skid concrete pavers
- Installation of networked traffic signaling poles with pedestrian countdown timers
- Road markings using thermoplastic paint


Eligibility Criteria:
The bidder must satisfy ALL of the following conditions:

1. Must have successfully completed at least two urban road development or civil infrastructure projects of a value not less than INR 10 Crores each in the last five years.
2. Average Annual Turnover of the bidder must be greater than INR 50 Crores over the last three financial years.
3. Must possess a valid Solvency Certificate from a nationalized bank.
4. Valid GSTIN registration in the state of Maharashtra.


Mandatory Certifications:
- ISO 14001:2015 certification is mandatory
- MSME or NSIC registered vendors will be given preference for EMD exemptions


Financial Requirement:
- Earnest Money Deposit (EMD): INR 35 Lakhs (demand draft)
- Performance Security: 3% of contract value
- Bid Validity: 90 days from submission date
